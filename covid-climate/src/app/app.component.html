CO2-Emissions

2019 & 2020

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the world looked a little bit different in 2020. People stayed at home, travelling was often impossible and planes had to stay grounded for the most part.

These factors have resulted in noticeable changes to CO2 emissions. As can be seen in the chart on the right, the daily CO2 emissions for 2020 look fairly different from those of last year.

  • 2020
